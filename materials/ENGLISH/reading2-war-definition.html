Reading 2: Definition of War

1

What are four types of war?

Answer

The four types of war are civil wars, religious wars, wars fought to gain territory, and wars of terror.

翻译：四种战争类型分别是：内战、宗教战争、领土争夺战争以及恐怖战争。

2

What is one effect of civil war?

Answer

One effect is that families may be separated because a son might fight for one side and a father fight for the other.

翻译：一个影响是家庭可能会因此分离，因为儿子可能会为其中一方作战，而父亲则在另一方作战。

3

What is an example of a religious war?

Answer

An example of a religious war is the Crusades, which were fought between Christians and Muslims between 1095 and 1291.

翻译：宗教战争的一个例子是十字军东征，这是在1095年至1291年间基督徒与穆斯林之间进行的战争。

4

What is an example of a war fought over territory?

Answer

An example is when Iraq invaded Kuwait in 1990 to take its oil fields.

翻译：一个例子是1990年伊拉克入侵科威特以占领其油田。

5

Which cities have been involved in the war of terror?

Answer

Cities such as New York, Washington D.C., Madrid, London, Mumbai, and Paris have been involved in the war of terror.

翻译：纽约、华盛顿特区、马德里、伦敦、孟买和巴黎等城市都曾卷入恐怖战争。

6

How is a war of terror different from a “typical” war?

Answer

Typical wars are fought by officially trained soldiers, while a war of terror is carried out by militant groups that use children as bombs. Furthermore, a war of terror aims to create intense fear and uses the media to spread their messages.

翻译：常规战争参战的是官方训练的士兵，而恐怖战争则由激进组织发起，会用小孩做炸弹。此外，恐怖战争旨在创造剧烈的恐惧，并且会利用媒体来传播他们的信息。

7

What is the role of media in war?

Answer

The media can be used to control what information people receive about a war, or it can be used to show the reality of war and influence public opinion.

翻译：媒体可以被用来控制人们接收到的战争信息，也可以被用来展示战争的现实并影响公众舆论。
