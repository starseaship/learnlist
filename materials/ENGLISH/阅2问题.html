Question 1

What does it mean that even though there is no war, there is also no peace?

Answer: It means that even if there is no war, people may still suffer from social issues such as poverty, prejudice, discrimination, or bullying.

意思是：即使没有战争，人们也可能因为贫困、偏见、歧视或霸凌等社会问题而受苦。

Question 2

What are three things that might cause people to suffer?

Answer: Prejudice, poverty, and discrimination might cause people to suffer.

三个例子：偏见、贫困、歧视。也可以把 bullying 写进去。

Question 3

Why are social issues difficult to fix?

Answer: Social issues are difficult to fix because they are very complicated and connected to many parts of society.

社会问题很难解决，因为它们很复杂，而且和社会的很多部分有关。

Question 4

What laws did South Africa have that caused people to suffer?

Answer: South Africa had apartheid laws. These laws separated people by race. For example, people of different races had to go to different schools and use different public places.

这里老师说需要例子，所以加入了学校和公共场所分开的例子。

Question 5

What does the sentence, “this has not eliminated differences in income and education levels” mean?

Answer: It means that even though racial equality became part of the constitution, there are still gaps in income and education levels between different groups.

意思是：法律上有了种族平等，但不同群体之间在收入和教育水平上仍然有差距。

Question 6

Why must we continue to try to solve the problems of social issues?

Answer: Because solving social issues takes a lot of time and effort, but we must solve them to get real peace.

因为解决社会问题需要时间和努力，但真正的和平需要解决这些问题。

Question 7

What does negative peace mean?

Answer: Negative peace means a situation in which there is no war or violence.

消极和平：没有战争、没有暴力。

Question 8

What does positive peace mean?

Answer: Positive peace means a situation in which there are no serious social issues. It also means dealing with social justice.

积极和平：不只是没有战争，还要减少社会问题，并处理社会正义的问题。

Question 9

What does positive peace include?

Answer: Positive peace includes economic justice, personal well-being, respect for human rights, inclusive institutions, and environmental sustainability.

积极和平包括：经济正义、个人福祉、尊重人权、包容性制度、环境可持续性。
