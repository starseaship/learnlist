Paragraph Structure 段落结构
1
Topic Sentence
Can explain what a topic sentence does.
能解释主题句的作用
Answer
It expresses the main idea of a paragraph and often tells the reader how many supporting points will follow.
表达段落的主旨，并通常告诉读者接下来会有几个支撑点。
2
Topic Sentence
Can explain the location of a topic sentence.
能解释主题句的位置
Answer
It is usually located at the first sentence of a paragraph — also called the introductory sentence or section.
通常是段落的第一句话，也叫做引入句（introductory sentence）。
Introductory Paragraph 引言段落
3
Thesis Statement
Can tell what a thesis statement does.
能说明论点句的作用
Answer
It expresses the main idea of an entire essay and states the writer's position and main points on the topic.
表达整篇文章的主旨，并说明作者的立场与主要观点。
4
Thesis Statement
Can explain the location of a thesis statement.
能解释论点句的位置
Answer
It is usually the last sentence of the introductory paragraph.
通常是引言段落的最后一句话。
5
Hook
Can explain the role of the hook.
能解释钩子句的作用
Answer
An opening sentence to create interest in the reader — it makes the reader want to continue reading.
一句吸引读者兴趣的开篇句，让读者想继续阅读下去。
6
Hook
Can explain the location of the hook.
能解释钩子句的位置
Answer
The first sentence (or first part) of the introductory paragraph.
引言段落的第一句（或开头部分）。
7
Hook
Can tell three techniques to use in the hook.
能说出钩子句的三种写法
Answer
A surprising fact An interesting question An imaginary situation
令人惊讶的事实、有趣的问题、假想情境
Transitions 过渡词
8
Transition Words
Can explain what transitions do.
能解释过渡词的作用
Answer
They connect ideas and help the reader see the logical relationships between them, allowing the paragraph to be organized in a clear, logical way.
连接观点，帮助读者看清各想法之间的逻辑关系，使段落结构清晰有序。
Concluding Paragraph 结论段落
9
Summary Statement
Can explain what a summary statement does.
能解释总结陈述句的作用
Answer
It paraphrases the thesis statement — the author restates the main idea of the essay in different words.
改写论点句——作者用不同的词重新表达文章主旨（不能原文照抄）。
10
Final Thought
Can tell two techniques to use in the final thought.
能说出结语的两种写法
Answer
Ask a question Make a prediction
提出问题　或　做出预测
