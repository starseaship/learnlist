Integrated Reading · Supplement Worksheet
What Do You Know About
Child Soldiers?
8 Questions · 知识点整理 + 中文注释
⚠
本笔记涉及儿童兵这一严肃人权议题，内容包含暴力、虐待等敏感信息，仅作学术学习整理之用。
Definition & Scale 定义与规模
1
Who are child soldiers?
什么是儿童兵？
Answer
Children (under 18) who are used for any military purpose.
泛指被用于任何军事目的的未满18岁儿童，不只限于直接参战。
2
How many child soldiers were used in South Sudan?
南苏丹有多少儿童兵？
Answer
At least 19,000 children are believed to be participating in the conflict in South Sudan.
据信有至少 19,000名儿童 参与了南苏丹冲突。
Geography 主要地区
3
Besides South Sudan, what are the main countries where child soldiers are being used?
除南苏丹外，还有哪些主要国家使用儿童兵？
Answer
Afghanistan Myanmar Somalia Sudan Syria Yemen
阿富汗、缅甸、索马里、苏丹、叙利亚、也门
Causes 原因与成因
4
Why are children used in wars?
为何儿童被用于战争？
Answer
Children are easier to manipulate than adults — they can be controlled and influenced more readily.
儿童比成人更容易被操控，思想尚未成熟，更容易被影响和利用。
5
How do they become child soldiers?
儿童如何成为儿童兵？
Answer
1
Easy to manipulate than adults.
2
Some are recruited by force (e.g. abduction 绑架).
3
Others choose to join to escape poverty, child abuse, or as a way of survival.
① 儿童易被操控　② 部分遭强制征募（如绑架）　③ 部分为逃离贫困、虐待或寻求生存而主动加入
Harm 危害
6
How is becoming a child soldier harmful?
成为儿童兵有什么危害？
Answer
1
High risk of being killed.
2
Risk of being sexually abused by adults.
3
Being traumatized afterward — PTSD (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder).
① 极高的死亡风险　② 遭受成人性虐待的风险　③ 事后留下心理创伤（PTSD：创伤后应激障碍）
Solutions 解决方式
7
What can we do to stop (recruiting) child soldiers?
我们能做什么来阻止招募儿童兵？
Answer
1
Support an organization that is working for child soldiers by donation.
2
Take part in events to help raise awareness.
① 通过捐款支持相关组织　② 参与活动以提高公众意识
Roles 儿童兵的角色
8
What roles do child soldiers play?
儿童兵承担哪些角色？
Answer
⚔️
Fighters
to kill and commit other acts of violence
🍳
Cooks
to prepare food for troops
📦
Porters
to carry weapons and injured soldiers
📨
Messengers
to deliver information
⚠️
Sexual slaves
both girls and boys
战斗员（施暴）、厨师、搬运工（运送武器和伤员）、信使、性奴（男女儿童均受害）
